Who am I?

Hi there! My name is Sened Haddad, I'm 21 years old, and I live in San Francisco. I graduated from St. Ignatius College Preparatory, and now I'm a senior Computer Science major at Santa Clara University (SCU). I am interested in software development, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data science. My only programming experience going into college was an introduction to computer science class that I took at a community college while I was in high school. After taking programming classes in C++ in school my freshman year at SCU, I began learning HTML and CSS by myself to build this website. After more coursework and job experience, I've been able to learn a lot more about computer science.

Outside of computer science, I enjoy staying active by playing soccer and basketball, lifting weights, going on runs, and traveling. I like watching sports, especially the Bay Area sports teams like the Warriors, 49ers, and SF Giants. I love learning new things, and my most recent endeavor was trying to learn how to read and write Arabic on my own. Lastly, I love listening to music! If you have any questions or want to talk, contact me!
