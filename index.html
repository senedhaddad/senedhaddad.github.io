About Me

Hi there! My name is Sened Haddad, and I was born, raised, and now live in San Francisco. I graduated from Santa Clara University, majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Mathematics. After completing my coursework and some industry experience, I've been able to learn a lot more about the technology world. I am most interested in software/app development, product management, and AI/ML fields. I am currently seeking opportunities to combine my technical, leadership, and soft skills in a full-time role.

Starting in May 2020, I helped my parents open our pizzeria Pizza Due in San Francisco during the COVID-19 pandemic. Although growing up learning a lot about entrepeneurship and business from my parents' success with Morning Due, this presented many new challenges from simply opening a new business during a pandemic to also balancing remote schoolwork, managing and leading a website redesign, and pushing Opal forward.

Outside of work, I enjoy staying active by playing soccer and basketball as well as lifting weights. I like watching sports, especially the Bay Area sports teams like the Warriors, 49ers, and SF Giants. I love learning new things. Some recent things I've enjoyed learning are reading & writing Arabic, graphic design on Figma, and now app development. Lastly, I love listening to music, mostly R&B, rap, and hip-hop! If you have any questions or want to talk, contact me!
